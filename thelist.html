Welcome to The List! You might be wondering, what the hell is THE LIST? Capital T, The. Not a list. Not some list. But The List. The List is more than a to-do. It is not just actions to take, but the small things which start to define the SYSTEMS WE LIVE BY. The List is comprised of the things that I want to achieve, complete, learn, overcome, and understand. From this list, I don't will not become someone who draws, someone or programs, or someone or exercises. I will become an artist, programmer, and an athlete. Many say that the time of the Renaissance man is over, the abundance of knowledge and information and skills to learn to too much. This is true. I cannot learn everything, BUT I CAN get very, very good at a LOT of things. We have around 80 years on this Earth, and I'll be damned if I'm not going to make the most of it. Welcome to The List!
